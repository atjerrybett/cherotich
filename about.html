🎨 Creating since age 10!

Hey, I'm Joy! 👋🇰🇪

I'm Joy Cherotich, a 15-year-old artist from Kenya. I've been making art since I was 10 — starting with crayons and sketch pads, and now working with acrylics, digital tools, and mixed media.

I built this website to show the world that age is just a number when it comes to creativity. There are SO many talented young artists in Kenya, and we deserve a platform to be seen. 💜

If you're a young creator too, I'd love to feature your work here. Let's build something amazing together!
